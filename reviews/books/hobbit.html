Few books have captured the imagination of readers like *The Hobbit*. First published in 1937, this classic fantasy novel follows the journey of Bilbo Baggins, an unassuming hobbit who is thrust into an epic adventure. What starts as a simple quest to help a band of dwarves reclaim their homeland turns into a transformative experience filled with magic, danger, and self-discovery.
