🎬 Movie Review: Inception

⬅ Back to Blog

🎬 Director: Christopher Nolan

🎭 Genre: Sci-Fi, Thriller

📅 Watched On: 10/02/25

⭐ Rating: 9/10

*Inception* is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller that takes viewers deep into the world of dreams. Directed by **Christopher Nolan**, the film follows Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio), a thief who specializes in stealing secrets from within the subconscious. Given a chance to erase his criminal past, he must plant an idea into someone's mind—a process known as inception.
