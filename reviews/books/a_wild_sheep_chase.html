Started reading this book after finishing Sputnik Sweetheart wanted to continue the Murakami vibes. This books starts more on the mysterious side and has you questioning the main character. In particular the first part of the book showing him meet this girl who he likes because of her ears is so fucking weird I love it. It does what I feel Murakami does best and thats making you question everything that is going on because it seems so strange. This book did the magical realism really well. Not as good as Kafka but still better than the other Murakami I've read. The title of the book literally describes what the book is about lol a wierd sheep chase that takes the main character to hokkaido. The description of the locations is so vivid I could really picture it. It had the classic tone of Murakami as well very slow and then crescendos at the end leaving unanswered questions as well as answering some that only lead to more questions. I really wish there was someone else I knew who had read this book to discuss it because thats one of the thing I think that makes GoT so special. The online community is goated and Murakami doesn't really have that. I think this is my second favourite Murakami book after Kafka and just reminds me how amazing writers minds are.
