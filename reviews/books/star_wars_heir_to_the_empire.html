Was inspired to read some star wars legends books after watching a fuck tonne of star wars content recently. I saw online that the thrawn series is one of the best star wars books so I thought I would check it out. Thrawn is so fucking cool. The way he reads situations as well as his machievellianism is so good. The fact that he studies a societies art to understand how they think and therefore read their actions is so good. I was also impressed that this book made me actually rate the original trilogy characters in particlarly Luke,Leia and Han. I didn't expect myself to be interested in the characters from the original trilogy but I was. I'm hoping the next book goes into more detail on Joruus C'baoth and the fact that he is a clone that is dark jedi. This book is especially good because it really explands on the original trilogy and is able to introduce new characters and locations and still retain the original trilogy feel. Also big up Lando the goat. I want to rewatch the original trilogy now.
