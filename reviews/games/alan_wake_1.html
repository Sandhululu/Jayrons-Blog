🎮 Game Review: Alan Wake

⬅ Back to Blog

📚 Developer: Remedy Entertainment

🎭 Genre: Action/Adventure

⏳ Release Date: 14th May 2010

⭐ Rating: 8/10

Quite an old game so some of the combat was definitely outdated. Hopefully these issues have been resolved for Alan Wake 2, which I will play after I upgrade my computer. The strongest part of this game is the storytelling and the lore around it. This is something that I find that Remedy Entertainment do very well. In particular the story around the manuscripts and the fact that alan is playing through his own story to be a great concept. Thinking back on this game I'm still confused lol. I should probably play this game back or at least watch a yt vid before I play Alan Wake 2.
