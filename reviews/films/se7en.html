🎬 Movie Review: Se7en

⬅ Back to Blog

🎬 Director: David Fincher

🎭 Genre: Crime, Horror, Thriller

📅 Watched On: 29/03/2025

⭐ Rating: 8.5/10

Watched this hungover after the conducta rave so probably missed a lot of the small details. Really good acting Brad Pitt, Morgan Freeman and Kevin Spacey. The best bit of this movie was definitely the end with how Spacey's villain controlled the entirety of the movie and even the end as well. Once it became clear what was going to happen I felt bad for Pitt's character but due to how his character was developed throughout the movie it was clear that he was going to kill him. This movie addressed many fascinating themes throughout in particular, the 7 deadly sins, complacency and denial, martrydom. The depth of the characters and how they relate to these earlier mentioned themes is the undeniably biggest strength. You know a movie is good when you go to youtube after to see all the little small details and character breakdown that you missed.
