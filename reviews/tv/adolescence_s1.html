This tv show is only 4 episodes long. Each episode focusing on a different group of people. The cinematography of this tv show is the high point as well as some good performances by the actors. In particular the kid actor who plays the main actor really did a good job of making people hate him. Reminds me a bit of Jack Gleeson but not quite as good. The fact that the episodes are recorded in one take is also hella good. Definitely a tear jerker this one. In particular seeing the impact of this kids actions on the ones around him and the affected. Where this tv show falls short is that it doesn't explain very much why the kid does what he does. Apparently he murders the girl because of some mean comments on his instagram. Like thats a bit far fetched. Would have definitely been a better watch if it went more into what caused the kid to do the murder. Feels like it was made more for the older generations who have no idea what an incel/online stuff works.
