📜 Recipe Summary

This **Creamy Garlic Pasta** is a simple yet delicious recipe made with fresh garlic, parmesan cheese, and a rich, creamy sauce. It’s the perfect dish for a quick weeknight dinner that feels gourmet! The balance between garlic and cream is spot on, making it a must-try for pasta lovers.

🍽 Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream, stirring continuously, then mix in parmesan cheese.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then toss the pasta in the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley. Enjoy!

📌 Final Thoughts

This pasta recipe is a **solid 9/10**—it's easy to make, flavorful, and super creamy. The garlic flavor is well-balanced, and the parmesan adds a lovely depth. Perfect for a cozy dinner at home!
